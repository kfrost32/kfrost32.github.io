Better Place Forests

I worked with BPF's tech lead to design and build internal applications that expedite the sales process, and customer-facing tools that help a unique customer base (60+ yrs old) sign online contracts and use online payments.

The primary challenge for Better Place was developing a system that allowed the business to scale, and at the same time minimizing the need to scale a sales force. We focused on researching and building an online tool that would allow a small team to sell to our unique customer base with as little hands-on as possible. Since launch, the application has processed tens of thousands of dollars in revenue, mostly from consumers who have little to no experience with online payments or online contracts.

"Easiest process I’ve ever used online!"

Ann Long, age 72 - after using BFP's online contract & payment process for the first time.

Another significant portion of the project was developing internal dashboards that allowed the sales team to quickly evaluate inventory and manage contracts and tree-selections in progress.
